Frequently Asked Questions

These frequently asked questions will be able to help guide you and answer your questions.
If you encounter any other issues, please do not hesitate to contact us.

How to contact the company?

Contact us through our number +9731789098. You can also fill the contact us form given on the website or click on the following button to access the form.

How to order from the website?

Just choose the bouquet you would like and add it to your cart. You can place your order and checkout from the cart page. You can also select bouquets based on the occassion.

What is the delivery charge and payment method?

We offer free delivery to all areas in Bahrain. Currently, we only deliver to Bahrain. We accept credit and debit cards, we also provide cash on delivery.

The flowers I ordered do not look like the flowers I recieved. What do I do?

We take great care when designing the flower arrangements available on our website. We make sure to deliver bouquets that resemble the image on the website, but unfortunately this is not always possible as our florists may sometimes need to make last-minute changes to the flowers that they use to make up the arrangements. This may be due to stock availability in their area or due to the flowers required for a particular arrangement not being of a high enough standard. In these cases, our florists will substitute the necessary flowers with others of a similar style or colour to your original order. You will also recieve a coupon of 50% off for your next order.
